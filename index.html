Introduction

The Science of Analogies have granted to this Earth America and to the sky the planets of Le Verrier. This science resembles those women, born to reign in balls and in theatres, women whose beauty is contested by all, except when all fall in love with them at the first opportunity. Eternal and forever the young heir of Plato, the Science of Analogies with rainbow-colored wings batters down the last barriers to human knowledge, while Experimental Science , tobacco-infused contemporary of Galileo, stumbles, despite her glasses, on the pebbles of mail route .
